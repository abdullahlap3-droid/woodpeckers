Our Craftsmanship

Master Woodworkers Since 1995

Woodpeckers Cabinets has been creating custom cabinetry for over 25 years. Our passion for woodworking is evident in every piece we create, from kitchen cabinets to specialty cigar humidors.

We use only the finest hardwoods and materials, combining traditional joinery techniques with modern precision tools to create pieces that are both beautiful and built to last.

Our team of craftsmen takes pride in their work, ensuring that each cabinet is not just furniture, but a work of art that will enhance your home for generations.

Cigar Cabinets & Humidors

Our specialty cigar cabinets are designed for the discerning collector. Each humidor is crafted with Spanish cedar interiors and precision humidity control systems to preserve your valuable collection.

We offer custom sizing and configurations, from small desktop humidors to walk-in cigar rooms. Our cabinets feature beautiful exterior finishes that complement any decor while providing optimal conditions for cigar storage.
